It is said that one century ago, after decades of adventures, the sorcerer Astragad the Reddish used his army of minions to build a giant tower, where he could rest and store his many treasures. Though he died a long time ago, his dungeon remains, filled with gold for you to claim. But beware, for this place is protected by an army of monsters, deadly traps, and a powerful magic that defies time and space. 

What price are you ready to pay for entering this mighty dungeon?
